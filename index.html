Twitter Bootstrap Integrated with Select2 and Chosen

Twitter Bootstrap 3.0, integrate and extended default <select> UI with jQuery Select2 and Chosen.

For those who bored with default UI, here's Twitter Bootstrap 3.0 that I've integrate and extended with "Select2" and "Chosen". Enjoy and do share :)
